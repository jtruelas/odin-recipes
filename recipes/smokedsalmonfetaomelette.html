Smoked Salmon Feta Omelette with Toast

Serving Size: 1

5 minutes

  • 2 Eggs
  • 60 grams Egg Whites (1/4 Cup)
  • 2 tbsps Fresh Dill (finely chopped)
  • 10 grams Feta Cheese (1 tbsp, crumbled)
  • 85 grams Smoked Salmon (3oz)
  • 3 slices Whole Grain Bread
  • 3 tbsps Low Sugar Jam
Nutrition Facts
AMOUNT PER SERVING
Calories 622 Protein 45g
Fat 20g Sodium 1399mg
Carbs 61g Potassium 716mg
Fiber 10g
  1. In a bowl, add the eggs, egg whites, and half of the dill. Whisk well to combine.
  2. In a non-stick skillet over medium heat, spray with non-stick spray.
  3. Once hout, pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook unitl almost set. Place the feta cheese on one half of the omelette and then fold the other half over on top. Remove from the heat and transfer onto a plate.
  4. Meanwhile, toast the bread slices. Once toasted, spread jam over top.
  5. Top omelette with smoked salmon and the remaining dill. Serve with toast on the side. Enjoy!
