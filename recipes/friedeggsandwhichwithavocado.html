Fried Egg Sandwich with Avocado & Fruit

Serving Size: 1

10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/8 tsp Sea Salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 slices Whole Grain Bread
  • 130 grams Egg Whites (1/4 Cup)
  • 40 grams Avocado (1/4 avocado, sliced)
  • 200 grams Apple

Nutritional Facts

AMOUNT PER SERVING
Calories 610 Protein 40g
Fat 24g Sodium 1043mg
Carbs 59g Potassium 1017mg
Fiber 14g

Directions

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat and spray with a non-stick spray. Cook 2 eggs until set. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from pan and add egg whites and remaining egg.
  2. While the egg mixture cooks, toast the bread and add the sliced avocado onto it.
  3. Add the eggs, close the sandwhich and slice. Enjoy with extra scrambled eggs and apple on the side!
